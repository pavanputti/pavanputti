## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📄 [Legal Document Assistant](https://github.com/pavanputti/legal-doc-assistant) · [Live Demo](http://legal-doc-assistant-naga-pavan-puttis-projects.vercel.app/)
AI-powered web app that uploads legal document templates, detects placeholders conversationally, and returns completed documents with formatting preserved. Built to help people who can't afford legal assistance.

`React` `Vite` `Zustand` `Hugging Face` `Firebase` `PizZip`

---

### 🤖 [Post-Meeting Social Content Generator](https://github.com/pavanputti/post-meeting-social-content-generator)
Joins meetings via Recall.ai bot, pulls transcripts, and auto-generates and posts content to LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter/X. Integrates Google Calendar, OAuth, PostgreSQL, and Vercel cron jobs.

`Next.js 14` `TypeScript` `PostgreSQL` `Prisma` `Gemini API` `Recall.ai`
